## Header (Top-Level Navigation)

The main navigation menu (sticky on desktop) includes:

- Home
- The Return™
- The Pathway
- Programs
- Blog
- Newsletter
- Contact

## Footer (The Cinematic Footer)

The footer uses a 3-column layout with a minimal, cinematic aesthetic (black background, padding M).

**Column 1: Navigation**
- The Return™
- The Pathway
- Programs
- Blog
- Newsletter
- Contact

**Column 2: Programs**
- The Return™
- The Purpose Method™
- The Trilogy™
- The Circle™

**Column 3: Legal**
- Legal & Policy Information

**Bottom Center**
- Brand Icon

### Footer Typography & Styling Rules
- Keep footer links minimal.
- Typography: bold, uppercase for headers; thin, spaced for subheaders/links.
- Background: black.
- Layout: wide spacing, slow pacing, cinematic breathing room.
